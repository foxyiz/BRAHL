Questions? Answered.

What is QA on Air?

A peer-to-peer marketplace connecting Creators who need apps tested with QA Hunters who deliver results — powered by the BRAHL lifecycle and FoXYiZ.

What is BRAHL?

Build · Run · Analyze · Heal · Loop — the continuous quality cycle. On the BRAHL tab you also coordinate with your project team while reports and Go/No-Go stay in view.

Who can join?

Creators, QA Hunters, Promoters, and Nalanda learners. Start from a role on this page, or redeem an invite for a 7-day trial.

How do I get paid?

QA Hunters earn toward payout from challenge wallets. See Pricing for membership, wallets, and hunter earnings.
